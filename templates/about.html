LinkTor Tutorial Services is a universal tutorial platform that connects students to tutors on campus who have been assessed, trained and certified, who would teach them effectively at their own convenience boosting the performance of the student by focusing more on their weak areas. Also we connect students to tutors whose personalities complement each other using a systematic teaching method that would help improve the performance of the student and make them excel in their exams. Also at LinkTor, we aim to empower students, helping them relate with each other better, mentoring, learning and making money so they can be financially independent on campus and so they can study effectively.

How it works / student category

  • signs up with LinkTor in the STUDENT category

  • Log in to edit profile and also changing default password and username sent to email

  • Requests for a tutor

  • Tutor would be assigned within 24hours of request after validation of fee payment


How it works / Tutor category

  • signs up with LinkTor either in the TUTOR category

  • Selects course to tutor

  • Gets an interview date with the linktor team

  • Recieves link to login and edit details after succesfull interview

  • Gets renumurated after successfull tuition(based on after-srvice payment options selected by tutor)
